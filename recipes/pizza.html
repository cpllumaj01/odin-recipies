Pizza Recipe

This is what a pizza will look like when it is finished being cooked

This pizza will be ultimately very delicious, there is no reason the pizza should not be eaten as it will be very good, not healthy but delicious.

Ingredients:

  • cheese
  • sauce
  • dough

Steps:

  1. Flatten the dough
  2. Put sauce and cheese on the dough
  3. Cook the pizza and eat it!!!
